Introduction

I'm Brent Buenarte, an IT professional with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Western Governors University. I focus on translating my rigorous academic foundation into practical, scalable solutions across the full development lifecycle.

My current work involves creating, improving, and maintaining both new and existing technology projects, from critical bug fixing to continuous feature implementation. Outside of my technical endeavors, I pursue creativity through drawing, enjoy strategic problem-solving in video games, and have a strong passion for food.

My core technical expertise, acquired through focused coursework and necessary certifications, includes the following programming/query languages and frameworks:

  • Axelos ITIL 4 Information Technology Management
  • AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner
